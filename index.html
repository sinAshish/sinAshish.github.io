Bio

I graduated in 2020 with a Bachelors in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee where I was advised by Prof. K.S Suresh for my thesis.
My research interests lie in 2D/ 3D vision for scene understanding, view synthesis and computational photography using un/semi/weakly-supervised algorithms. I am also passionate about Materials informatics and speech processing.
Currently, I am working with Prof. Jonghyun Choi on point cloud analysis. For my Bachelors' thesis, I worked on Localizing and Classfiying surface defects in surface and Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM) images of metals. During the summers of my junior year, I interned at Preferred Networks where I worked on 3D GAN-based reconstruction. I am also fortunate to have been supervised by Prof. Jose Dolz on biomedical image analysis.

I am currently applying for Grad schools while working full-time at Wells Fargo.
If you want to collaborate or just chat, the best way to reach me is via email.
